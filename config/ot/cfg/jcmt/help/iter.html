Sequences and Iterators in the OT

Iterators and a feature of the OT that provide a powerful tool for creating sequences in which observations of various types are taken while changing instrument configurations, telescope positions, etc.

They have a structure like nested do loops. It is important therefore to make sure that "Observe" is in the right place in the hierarchy.
